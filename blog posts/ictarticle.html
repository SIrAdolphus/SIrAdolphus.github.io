The Future of AI in ICT: What's Next?

Artificial Intelligence (AI) is rapidly transforming the field of Information and Communication Technology (ICT). As AI continues to advance, it is set to redefine how we interact with technology, making systems smarter, more efficient, and more responsive to our needs.
