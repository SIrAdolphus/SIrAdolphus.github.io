Frequently Asked Questions!
How can you book a Project space?

Step 1! - Go to your HZ Portal!
Step 2! - Go to the self service
Step 3! - Click on reserve
Step 4! - Choose the correct category and search for the "recource" you want to reserve.
Step 5! - Select the day you're reserving this recource.
Step 6! - By making a box over the time table indicate what time you will be reserving.
Step 7! - Click 'Next'
Step 8! - Read the conditions for borrowing, Check the reservation and click next.
Step 9! - Fill in the required information and click confirm reservation.

How can you print a document from your laptop at HZ?

Step 1! - Go to https://Mypaynet.nl/ or scan the qr by the printer.
Step 2! - CLick on 'web print'
Step 3! - upload your files and pick them up at the printer.

What are the instructions for parking your car at HZ Middelburg?

Step 1! - Park your car in the parking garage named 'Parkeergarage Kousteensedijk'.
Step 2! - Walk towards the HZ building and do the things you cam to do!
Step 3! - Walk in to the JRCZ and ask for a exit ticket at the counter. (you may have to show your school pass)
Step 4! - Walk towards your car and drive away!

How can you scan a document and send it to your laptop at HZ?

Step 1! - Scan your card at one of the various printers within HZ.
Step 2! - Open the printer and put the paper you want to scan on the glassplate by the corner.
Step 3! - close the printer and select scan.
step 4! - follow the steps seen on the printer and press start.
Step 5! - Don't forget to log out!

How can I buy/sign-up something through th HZ Webshop?

Step 1! - Go towards the HZ Webshop!
Step 2! - Add the product/activities you want to your basket!
Step 3! - Double check in your cart if you have everyting and the correct things.
Step 4! - Order your products!
